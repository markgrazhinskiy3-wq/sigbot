1. PRIMARY TIMEFRAME: 1-minute candles (NOT 15-second)
   - Fetch last 50 candles of 1-min TF
   - Fetch last 20 candles of 5-min TF for context
   - 15-second candles are TOO NOISY for pattern analysis

2. STRATEGY-FIRST APPROACH:
   - Do NOT run all modules in parallel and blend scores
   - First detect market mode → then run ONLY relevant strategies
   - Each strategy has specific entry conditions that must be met
   - A strategy either triggers or it doesn't — no partial blending

3. NO SCORE AVERAGING:
   - Do NOT blend best×0.7 + average×0.3
   - Take the best strategy signal
   - If a second strategy confirms same direction → bonus +10
   - If strategies conflict → reduce confidence ×0.7

4. PENALTIES AS MULTIPLIERS NOT SUBTRACTION:
   - Old: -15, -8, -10 cumulative (could reach -55)

ARCHITECTURE — 4 LAYERS
════════════════════════════════

LAYER 1 — DATA COLLECTION
─────────────────────────
- 50 candles × 1-min timeframe (OHLC) — primary analysis
- 20 candles × 5-min timeframe (OHLC) — trend context
- Cache refresh every 20-30 seconds
- Calculate indicators on 1-min data:
  • EMA(5), EMA(13), EMA(21)
  • RSI(7)
  • Stochastic(5, 3, 3)
  • Momentum(5)
  • ATR(10)
  • Bollinger Bands(15, 2.0)
- Calculate on 5-min data:
  • EMA(21), EMA(50) — for trend context only
- Detect support/resistance levels:
  • Swing highs and lows with window of 3 bars on 1-min
  • Cluster within 0.15% tolerance
  • Also detect levels on 5-min (stronger levels)
  • A level needs 2+ touches to be significant
- Candle analysis:
  • Body size, shadow sizes, body/range ratio
  • Average body over last 10 candles for comparison

LAYER 2 — MARKET MODE DETECTION
────────────────────────────────
Determine current market state BEFORE running strategies.
This decides WHICH strategies to activate.

Modes:

TRENDING_UP:
  - EMA(5) > EMA(13) > EMA(21) on 1-min for last 5+ candles
  - EMA(21) > EMA(50) on 5-min
  - Last 5-7 candles predominantly bullish (60%+)
  - ATR stable or rising
  - Swing structure: higher highs + higher lows

TRENDING_DOWN:
  - EMA(5) < EMA(13) < EMA(21) on 1-min
  - EMA(21) < EMA(50) on 5-min
  - Last 5-7 candles predominantly bearish
  - Lower highs + lower lows

RANGE:
  - EMAs intertwined (spread between EMA5 and EMA21 < 0.03%)
  - Price oscillating between identifiable support and resistance
  - ATR low or medium
  - No consistent swing direction

VOLATILE:
  - ATR(10) > average ATR over 30 candles × 1.5
  - Large candle bodies AND large shadows
  - No clear directional structure
  - Frequent direction changes

SQUEEZE:
  - ATR(10) < average ATR over 30 candles × 0.6
  - Bollinger Bands narrowing (bandwidth decreasing)
  - Small candle bodies (< 50% of average)
  - Market storing energy before breakout

Strategy activation by mode:
  TRENDING_UP/DOWN → Strategy 1 (EMA Bounce) + Strategy 2 (Squeeze Breakout)
  RANGE → Strategy 3 (Level Bounce) + Strategy 4 (RSI Reversal)
  VOLATILE → Strategy 5 (Micro Breakout) + Strategy 6 (Divergence)
  SQUEEZE → Strategy 2 (Squeeze Breakout) — wait for breakout candle

IMPORTANT: In any mode, if a non-primary strategy has a very strong
signal (confidence > 80 with all conditions met), it can still trigger.
Mode filtering is a preference, not a hard block.

LAYER 3 — STRATEGIES (CORE ENGINE)
═══════════════════════════════════

Each strategy is a COMPLETE MARKET SCENARIO.
It describes what must happen on the chart for entry.
Indicators inside each strategy are measurement tools, not decision makers.

Each strategy returns:
  - direction: BUY / SELL / NONE
  - confidence: 0-100
  - conditions_met: X out of Y
  - reasoning: text explanation

A strategy returns NONE if fewer than 60% of its conditions are met.

──────────────────────────────────────────
STRATEGY 1 — EMA BOUNCE IN MICRO-TREND
──────────────────────────────────────────
Scenario: Price is trending. It pulls back to EMA(13) or EMA(21)
and bounces, continuing the trend. We catch the bounce for 1-2 candles.

What must happen on the chart:
  1. Clear micro-trend visible (3-5 impulse candles in one direction)
  2. Pullback of 2-3 smaller candles toward EMA
  3. Price touches or gets very close to EMA(13) or EMA(21)
  4. Bounce candle appears — closes in trend direction

Conditions for BUY (7 total):
  □ EMA(5) > EMA(13) > EMA(21) for last 5 candles — trend is aligned
  □ Price low touched EMA(13) zone — low <= EMA(13) × 1.0005
  □ Candle closed ABOVE EMA(13) — bounce confirmed
  □ Bounce candle is bullish with body > 50% of avg body
  □ Previous 2-3 candles were pullback (small or bearish)
  □ RSI(7) between 40 and 70
  □ Stochastic %K crossed above %D or %K > %D

Conditions for SELL: mirror all conditions

Confidence calculation:
  Base: conditions_met / total_conditions × 85
  Bonuses:
    +7 if 5-min trend confirms direction
    +5 if pullback touched EMA(13) precisely (within 0.02%)
    +3 if bounce candle has small shadow against trend (<20% of body)
    +3 if RSI in sweet spot (45-60 for BUY, 40-55 for SELL)

  Rejection (return NONE):
    - Pullback broke below EMA(21) and closed there — trend broken
    - RSI > 78 for BUY or RSI < 22 for SELL — exhausted
    - Price within 0.08% of strong opposite level — no room

──────────────────────────────────────────
STRATEGY 2 — SQUEEZE BREAKOUT
──────────────────────────────────────────
Scenario: Market compressed (small candles, low ATR, narrow Bollinger Bands).
Then a large impulse candle breaks out. We enter in the breakout direction
because momentum carries 1-3 more candles.

What must happen on the chart:
  1. Quiet period: 5-10 candles with small bodies
  2. Volatility compressed (ATR below normal, BB narrow)
  3. Breakout candle: body much larger than recent candles
  4. Candle closes beyond Bollinger Band or recent range

Conditions for BUY (7 total):
  □ ATR(10) of last 5 candles < avg ATR over 30 candles × 0.7
  □ Last 5-8 candle bodies < 60% of avg body over 30 candles
  □ Current candle is bullish with body > 2× avg body of last 10
  □ Close > upper Bollinger Band OR close > highest high of last 8 candles
  □ Shadow against direction < 30% of total candle range
  □ Momentum(5) > 0 and increasing
  □ EMA(5) turning up or already above EMA(13)

Conditions for SELL: mirror

Confidence calculation:
  Base: conditions_met / total_conditions × 80
  Bonuses:
    +7 if 5-min trend confirms direction
    +5 if squeeze lasted 8+ candles
    +5 if candle broke Bollinger Band decisively
    +3 if Momentum surged (> 2× previous value)

  Rejection (return NONE):
    - Breakout candle has shadow > 50% of body against direction
    - Breakout runs directly into a strong S/R level within 0.05%
    - RSI already extreme before breakout (>82 for BUY, <18 for SELL)

──────────────────────────────────────────
STRATEGY 3 — LEVEL BOUNCE
──────────────────────────────────────────
Scenario: Price reaches a significant S/R level and forms a reversal
candle pattern. Price bounces away from the level. Best in RANGE mode.

What must happen on the chart:
  1. Significant level identified (2+ previous touches)
  2. Price approaches level (within 0.1%)
  3. Reversal candle pattern appears AT the level
  4. Price starts moving away from the level

Reversal patterns to detect:

  Pin Bar: shadow toward level > 2× body, small body on opposite side
  Engulfing: small candle toward level → large candle away from level
  Hammer: small body, shadow toward level > 2× body, minimal opposite shadow

Conditions for BUY — bounce from support (7 total):
  □ Support level identified with 2+ touches
  □ Price low within 0.1% of support level in last 2 candles
  □ Reversal pattern detected (pin bar OR engulfing OR hammer)
  □ Current candle closed ABOVE support level
  □ RSI(7) < 35 — oversold confirms bottom
  □ Stochastic %K < 25 and turning up (%K > %D or %K rising)
  □ Distance to nearest resistance > 0.15% — room to move

Conditions for SELL: mirror from resistance

Confidence calculation:
  Base depends on pattern quality:
    Pin bar with shadow > 3× body → start at 85
    Engulfing with body > 1.5× previous → start at 80
    Hammer → start at 75
  Bonuses:
    +7 if level confirmed on both 1-min and 5-min
    +5 if level has 3+ touches
    +5 if market mode is RANGE
    +3 if RSI confirms extreme
    +3 if Stochastic confirms turn

  Rejection (return NONE):
    - No recognizable reversal pattern at the level
    - Level was broken previously and price returned (weakened)
    - Strong trend against bounce on 5-min (EMA21 steeply against us)
    - Distance to opposite level < 0.1% — nowhere to go

──────────────────────────────────────────
STRATEGY 4 — RSI EXTREME REVERSAL
──────────────────────────────────────────
Scenario: Price moved sharply, RSI hit extreme (<15 or >85).
A snapback of 1-3 candles is likely. We catch that snapback.

What must happen on the chart:
  1. Strong one-directional move (3-5 candles same direction)
  2. RSI(7) reached extreme territory
  3. First reversal or slowdown candle appears
  4. Stochastic confirms the turn

Conditions for BUY — snapback from oversold (6 total):
  □ RSI(7) was below 15 within last 3 candles
  □ Current RSI(7) > previous candle's RSI (starting to recover)
  □ Last 3-5 candles were bearish (the drop that caused oversold)
  □ Current candle is bullish OR has lower shadow > 2× body
  □ Stochastic %K < 20 and crossing %D upward
  □ Momentum(5) starting to rise

Conditions for SELL: mirror (RSI > 85)

Confidence calculation:
  RSI was below 10 → base = 85
  RSI was below 15 → base = 75
  RSI was below 20 → base = 65
  Bonuses:
    +7 if engulfing pattern on reversal candle
    +5 if pin bar on reversal
    +5 if support level nearby (for BUY)
    +5 if 5-min trend supports the snapback direction

  Rejection (return NONE):
    - Strong trend against snapback on 5-min AND RSI only reached 20 (not extreme enough)
    - No reversal candle — RSI extreme but price still pushing hard
    - ATR is extremely high (> 2.5× normal) — could be news event, unpredictable

──────────────────────────────────────────
STRATEGY 5 — MICRO LEVEL BREAKOUT
──────────────────────────────────────────
Scenario: Price tested a level 2-3 times and finally breaks through
with a strong candle. Breakout momentum carries 2-5 candles.

What must happen on the chart:
  1. Level with 2-3 touches in last 15-25 candles
  2. Each touch was a rejection (price bounced back)
  3. Breakout candle closes beyond the level with conviction
  4. No immediate reversal (price holds beyond level)

Conditions for BUY — breakout of resistance (7 total):
  □ Resistance with 2+ touches identified
  □ Current candle closed ABOVE resistance
  □ Breakout candle body > 1.5× avg body of last 10 candles
  □ Shadow against direction (upper for BUY) < 30% of body
  □ Momentum(5) > 0 and rising
  □ ATR(10) rising — energy behind the move
  □ EMA(5) and EMA(13) pointing in breakout direction

Conditions for SELL: mirror

Confidence calculation:
  Base: conditions_met / total_conditions × 80
  Bonuses:
    +7 if 5-min trend confirms breakout direction
    +5 if level had 3+ touches before breaking
    +3 if price closed 0.05%+ beyond level (strong hold)

  Rejection (return NONE):
    - Breakout against 5-min trend AND breakout candle body < 2× avg
    - Shadow against direction > 50% of body (weak breakout)
    - Another strong level within 0.05% beyond the broken one
    - ATR dropping — no energy for continuation

──────────────────────────────────────────
STRATEGY 6 — MICRO DIVERGENCE
──────────────────────────────────────────
Scenario: Price makes a new low/high but RSI does not confirm.
Movement is exhausting. Snapback of 1-3 candles expected.

What must happen on the chart:
  1. Two sequential price lows (or highs) within 10-20 candles
  2. Second extreme is more extreme than first
  3. RSI at second extreme is LESS extreme than at first (divergence)
  4. Reversal candle appears after second extreme

Conditions for BUY — bullish divergence (5 total):
  □ Two price lows found, second lower than first
  □ RSI(7) at second low > RSI at first low (bullish divergence)
  □ RSI difference between two points > 5 (meaningful divergence)
  □ Bullish candle appeared after second low
  □ Stochastic turning up

Conditions for SELL: mirror (bearish divergence)

Confidence calculation:
  Base = 75
  Bonuses:
    +10 if divergence aligns with a support/resistance level
    +7 if divergence visible on both RSI and Momentum
    +5 if market in RANGE mode
    +5 if 5-min trend supports divergence direction

  Rejection (return NONE):
    - Very strong trend against divergence on 5-min
    - No reversal candle — divergence present but no reaction yet
    - RSI difference < 5 between two extremes — too weak

LAYER 4 — SIGNAL DECISION ENGINE
════════════════════════════════════════

Step 1: Detect market mode

Step 2: Select strategies for current mode
  Primary strategies (run first, preferred):
    TRENDING → Strategy 1, Strategy 2
    RANGE → Strategy 3, Strategy 4
    VOLATILE → Strategy 5, Strategy 6
    SQUEEZE → Strategy 2

  Secondary strategies (run if primary gives no signal):
    All other strategies — but they need confidence > 80 to override

Step 3: Run selected strategies, collect results

Step 4: Select best signal
  - Take strategy with highest confidence where conditions_met >= 60%
  - If no strategy has conditions_met >= 60% → NO_SIGNAL

Step 5: Check for confirmation from other strategies
  - Another strategy agrees on direction → confidence + 10
  - Another strategy disagrees (opposite direction with conf > 50) → confidence × 0.75
  - No other strategy has an opinion → no change

Step 6: Apply contextual multipliers (NOT subtraction)
  All multipliers stack multiplicatively with a floor of ×0.60 total:

  Signal aligns with 5-min trend → ×1.10 (bonus)
  Signal against 5-min trend → ×0.82
  Market mode is chaotic/unclear → ×0.85
  Price within 0.1% of opposite level → ×0.85
  ATR is extremely high (>2.5× normal) → ×0.85
  ATR is compressed (<0.5× normal) AND strategy is not Squeeze Breakout → ×0.90

  Total multiplier = product of all applicable, but never below 0.60
  final_confidence = base_confidence × total_multiplier

Step 7: Final decision

  if final_confidence >= 70:
      signal = STRONG (3-5 stars)
      if final_confidence >= 88: stars = 5
      elif final_confidence >= 80: stars = 4
      else: stars = 3
      expiry = 1 min for bounce/breakout, 2 min for trend strategies

  elif final_confidence >= 52:
      signal = MODERATE (2 stars)
      expiry = 2 min

  else:
      signal = NO_SIGNAL

Step 8: Hard blocks (even with high confidence):
  - All strategies returned NONE → NO_SIGNAL
  - Best strategy conditions_met < 50% → NO_SIGNAL
  - Market mode SQUEEZE and no breakout candle detected → NO_SIGNAL
