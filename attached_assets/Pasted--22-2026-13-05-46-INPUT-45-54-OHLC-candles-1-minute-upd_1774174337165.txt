...## INPUT
- 45–54 OHLC candles (1-minute), updated every 45 seconds
- Analysis runs on the latest ~50 candles

## STEP 1 — MARKET REGIME DETECTION
Classify market into one of: uptrend / downtrend / range / weak_trend / chaotic_noise
- Use EMA(9) vs EMA(21) slope and price position
- Use ATR(14) relative to recent average ATR for volatility context
- chaotic_noise = ATR spike + no clear EMA direction + price whipping both sides

## STEP 2 — THREE PATTERN MODULES (each scores BUY and SELL from 0–100)

### A. Impulse + Pullback
- Strong move (3+ candles same direction, body > 60% of range)
- Then pullback of 30–50% of the move
- Then reversal candle confirming resumption
- Score based on completeness of all three phases

### B. Level Rejection
- Identify support/resistance: recent swing highs/lows (last 30 candles), round numbers
- Price approaches within 0.1% of level
- Rejection candle: long wick toward level, close away from it
- Score based on wick/body ratio and number of prior touches

### C. False Breakout
- Price closes beyond level
- Next 1–2 candles return inside range
- NOTE: Do NOT apply "trading against trend" penalty to this pattern — it is inherently counter-directional

## STEP 3 — COMBINE PATTERN SCORES
pa_score = best_pattern × 0.70 + average_of_three × 0.30
(This requires partial agreement, not just one strong signal)

## STEP 4 — SUPPORTING MODULES

### Candle Strength (score 0–100)
- Last 3 candles: body/range ratio, direction consistency
- Neutral candles (doji, spinning tops) reduce score

### Indicator Confirmation (score 0–100)
- EMA(9/21): crossover or alignment with signal direction
- RSI(14): not in extreme opposite zone (e.g., don't BUY above 75)
- Stochastic: K/D cross or direction
- Momentum: positive/negative
- Weight each 25%, combine into indicator_score

### Level Proximity (score 0–100, INVERSE)
- How far is price from the OPPOSITE key level
- Close to opposite level = lower score (risk of reversal)

## STEP 5 — CONFIDENCE FORMULA
confidence = pa_score×0.58 + candles×0.15 + regime×0.10 + indicators×0.12 + levels×0.05


## STEP 6 — PENALTIES
Apply these AFTER base confidence:
- weak_trend regime: −8
- Incomplete pattern (only 1–2 of 3 phases present): −8
- Trading against clear trend (NOT applicable to False Breakout pattern): −10
- Price within 0.08% of opposite level: −5
- Neutral/doji candles dominate last 3 bars: −5
- ATR compressed below 50% of 20-period average ATR: −4
- chaotic_noise regime: −15 (NOT a full block — apply penalty only)

## STEP 7 — HARD BLOCKS (signal suppressed regardless of score)
Only two hard blocks remain:
1. BUY and SELL scores differ by less than 3 points (true ambiguity)
2. All three pattern modules score below 30 on the winning side (no pattern support at all)

## STEP 8 — SIGNAL DECISION
| Confidence | Output |
|------------|--------|
| ≥ 68 | Strong signal — strength 3–5/5 (scale within 68–100) |
| 45–67 | Moderate signal — strength 2/5 |
| < 45 | NO_SIGNAL |

Signal includes: direction (BUY/SELL), strength (1–5), active pattern name, key reason in 1 line.

## DESIGN PRINCIPLES
- Prioritize giving signals in most market conditions — silence is not safe, it is lost opportunity
- chaotic_noise should penalize, not block — chaotic markets still have tradeable moments
- Pattern agreement matters more than one perfect module
- Indicators confirm, they do not lead — never let indicator_score alone push a signal over threshold...
